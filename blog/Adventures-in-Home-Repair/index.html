    Adventures in Home Repair

    The goal for today was to fix the front door knob and deadlock, as well as replace the bathroom faucet. Only one handle on the faucet is broken, but I pulled it all apart when Amanda was in China and the whole thing looks like it’s slowly falling apart. So instead of a band-aid fix I figured a new one was in order.

    Anyway, the plan was to get up early, head over to the DIY-Center and pick it all up in order to have the whole day to spend on pretending I know how to DIY.

    Unfortunately (or fortunately, given where my priorities were at the moment), I couldn’t help but slip back into bed after taking the poodles out and giving them a few bites to eat of their morning breakfast.

    So I had a late-start. Thankfully, the extra time spent in bed allowed me to check the Amazon order for the Poodle’s dog-food stand, which reminded me that the DIY-Center is right next to PetSmart. That led me to the PetSmart website where I found a much nicer dog-food stand. Everything happens for a reason, right?

    At around noon I finally ended up heading to PetSmart, picking up the dog-food stand and some treats, and then to the DIY-Center. I knew the knob/lock on our door was funky, but I had no idea what I was in for. Apparently it’s a Mortise? Some sort of antique knob/lock combo. And just happens to be completely incompatible with modern knobs and locks. So instead of getting the door and faucet fixed, I successfully disassembed and then reassembed the door.

    At which time I was missing Amanda, so I went to go spend time with her instead. That’s where I am now. Drinking wine and watching movie trailers with her. Not thinking about what I have to do tomorrow.
